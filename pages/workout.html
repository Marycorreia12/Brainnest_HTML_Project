Workout

Physical activity is a primary human function that is a prerequisite for good health throughout life.

According to research, one-third of adults in the world does not have a sufficiently active lifestyle. According to the latest reports in the European Union, six out of every ten people over the age of 15 have never engaged in physical activity or exercise or do it rarely. More than half of the people have never does in or have seldom been involved in a physical activity such as cycling, dancing or gardening. At the same time, a significant proportion of adults in Europe spend more than four hours a day sitting down, which may contribute to the formation of their sedentary lifestyle.
